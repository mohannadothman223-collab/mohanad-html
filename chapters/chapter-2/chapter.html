Plot Overview

Breaking Bad follows Walter White, a high‑school chemistry teacher who turns to manufacturing illegal substances after being diagnosed with cancer. Partnering with former student Jesse Pinkman, Walter enters a dangerous criminal world that transforms him into one of the most feared figures in the drug trade.
